Projects

Expresso

This past summer I worked with a team to ideate and finally present a high fideltiy prototype of a product that we came up with, that being Expresso. Expresso is an online efficient organization tool that digitizes the different properties of a bullet journal from calendars to journaling templates to to-do lists, while allowing a user to have the added benefits of accessibility, portability and efficiency while not losing the elements of creativity and customization. My work was primarily on ideating and implementing the design through prototyping.
