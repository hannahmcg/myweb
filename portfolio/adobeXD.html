AirBnb Workshop: "AirX."

Details: worked on a team of 3 people in designing a travelling app in which users could explore places to stay and create profiles based on various locations. Utilized Adobe XD to create a high fidelity prototype; our design featured an interactive map that highlighted where other users had created story profiles, as well as a music playlist feature that could be curated to one's trip.
