Product is live! Check it out: cssa.berkeley.edu

IDEATION

  • Met with organization members to compile list of content desired for the new website, organized by tabs. Discussed pain points and previous inconsistencies, and eventually were able to isolate direct goals, those being access to information and ease of use with an emphasis on acessibility on all fronts. Goals were designed with "improvement from previous website" in mind.
  • Used content to create low-fidelity mockups.

DESIGN

  • Held meetings to discuss color schemes, typography, and other design elements.
  • Used design templates and notes to create high-fidelity mockups of all potential website pages.
  • Continued to refine mockups and desired look and feel of new website, focusing on accessiblity (contrasting colors for clear visibility, easily readable buttons and links, etc.).

BUILD

  • Started initial HTML and CSS build early on to establish overall structure.
  • Once tabs and structure were set, incorporated Javascript elements for functionality and design (as with the particles.js component on the home page).
  • Continued to incoporate feedback on design and overall user data from organization members as well as potential users.
  • Build was complete over the course of five months (my fall semester of university).
